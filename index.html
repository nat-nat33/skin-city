Our Team

Face and Body Painting is not just for kid's birthday parties. Skin City's experienced and professional face and body painters create works of art on people at any age, any event, and any theme!

TIFFANY RODRIGUEZ

Face & Body Painting
– Owner, Lead Artist –

Owner and Lead Artist Tiffany Rodriguez has been creating works of art on people since 2012. She started out painting faces for friends and family during Halloween for fun. Since then she has grown and expanded her talent to bigger events including the Budwiser "Made in America" event held in Austin, TX. As a mother of 2 beautiful children herself she understands how important a birthday party or event can be to the host and makes it her mission to provide excellent, professional entertainment services to her clients. She leads a team of highly skilled artists who specialize in face and body art. Together they create amazing experiences for any occasion.

JOHNNIE ORTIZ

Face & Body Painting
– Artist –

Johnnie has been creating art ever since he could hold a utensil. His preferred form of media is acrylics on canvas but loves any opportunity to express himself creatively. As an aspiring Graphic Designer currently finishing up his senior year at SNHU for a bachelor’s in Graphic Design and Media Arts, art is his passion. He resides in Bastrop, TX but calls Austin, TX his home. When creating art, he loves experimenting with different ideas and new creative forms of expression. He seeks his inspiration from the cultural setting of central Texas, his Hispanic heritage and family. Above all, he enjoys making people happy with his art and loves when they find the beauty in it as well.

ROEL RIVERA

Face Painting
– Artist –

Roel has been Skin City’s go-to face painter for over 3 years. He has a unique style of face painting ensuring each client is comfortable and excited to transform into a different character. Roel’s greatest satisfaction is comes from the opportunity to develop unique works of art for each individual. Roel can take any idea and bring it to life. He loves to be challenged and try things he’s never done before.
